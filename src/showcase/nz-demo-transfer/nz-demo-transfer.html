Transfer

Double-bar shuttle selection box.

When to use #

Move elements intuitively in two columns to complete the selection behavior.

After selecting one or more options, click the corresponding arrow key to move the selected option to another column. The left column is source , the right column is target , and the API's design reflects both concepts.
