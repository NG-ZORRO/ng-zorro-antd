## zh-CN

通过使用 `nz-input` 并将 `nzVariant` 设置为 `outlined`、`underlined`、`filled` 和 `borderless`，即可支持变体

## en-US

Variant is supported out of the box by using `nz-input` and setting `nzVariant` to `outlined`, `underlined`, `filled` and `borderless`
