## zh-CN

按钮已接入[设计令牌](/docs/customize-theme-token/zh)体系：通过 `NzThemeService`（或启动时的 `provideNzTheme`）切换种子令牌、算法与组件令牌，按钮会实时响应。注意：主题作用于整个页面，离开本页时会自动恢复默认主题。

## en-US

The button consumes the [design token](/docs/customize-theme-token/en) system: switch seed tokens, algorithms and component tokens through `NzThemeService` (or `provideNzTheme` at bootstrap) and buttons respond instantly. Note: the theme applies to the whole page and is reset when you leave this page.
